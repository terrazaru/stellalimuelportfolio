Hello, I'm Stella.

I'm a fairly newcomer in the United States, coming from the country mostly known for paradise resorts to learn the merit of graphic design. While designing, I like to tune in to two robots making music. And when I'm not, I often find myself drawing, crafting, or finding wonders of the world.
