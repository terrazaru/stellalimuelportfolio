Light Again

Print

An annual report design for a non-profit organization, Running Strong for American Indian Youth®. The concept is developed following their mission, which aims to improve the life of Native American people. This is symbolized with a Native American story of an eagle who leads their people out of the darkness and into a valley of light. As one of the organization's goal is to pass the traditional customs to the new generation, the book is illustrated with pictographs and drawings inspired by their culture.
